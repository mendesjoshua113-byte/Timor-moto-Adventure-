About Timor Adventure Rides

Founded in 2015, Timor Adventure Rides is the premier dirt bike tour operator in Timor-Leste. We specialize in creating unforgettable off-road experiences that showcase the country's stunning landscapes, rich culture, and warm hospitality.

Our team of experienced local guides knows every trail, mountain pass, and hidden gem in Timor-Leste. We provide top-quality Honda dirt bikes, safety gear, and support vehicles to ensure your adventure is both thrilling and safe.

Whether you're a seasoned rider or a beginner looking for an exciting challenge, we have the perfect tour for you. Join us to discover the untamed beauty of Timor-Leste from the seat of a dirt bike!
